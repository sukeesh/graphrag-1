Inclusion in the Second Schedule to the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 – Uttarakhand State Cooperative Bank Ltd., Haldwani
We advise that the name of “Uttarakhand State Cooperative Bank Ltd., Haldwani" has been included in the Second Schedule to the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 by Notification DCBR.CO.RCBD.BC.No.03/19.51.025/2015-16 dated March 03, 2016 published in the Gazette of India (Weekly No. 20 - Part III - Section 4) dated May 14-May 20, 2016.
