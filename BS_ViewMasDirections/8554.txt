Formation of a new district in the State of Madhya Pradesh -
Assignment of Lead Bank Responsibility
The Government of Madhya Pradesh vide Notification No. F I-12-2005-VII-06 dated August 14, 2013 had  notified that the State Government has altered the limits of district Shajapur by excluding there from its tehsils, Agar, Barod, Susner and Nalkhera and  created a new district Agar-Malwa with effect from August 16, 2013 by comprising these tehsils.
