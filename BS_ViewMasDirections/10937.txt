Alteration in the name of "Coöperatieve Centrale Raiffeisen-Boerenleenbank B.A." to "Coöperatieve Rabobank U.A." in the Second Schedule to the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934
We advise that the name of "Coöperatieve Centrale Raiffeisen-Boerenleenbank B.A." has been changed to "Coöperatieve Rabobank U.A." in the Second Schedule to the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 by Notification DBR.IBD.No.11033/23.03.027/2015-16 dated March 23, 2016 and published in the Gazette of India (Part III Section 4) dated July 16, 2016.
