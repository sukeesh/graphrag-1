Notification
In exercise of the powers conferred by  sub-section (2A) of Section  24 of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (10 of 1949) and, in partial  modification of the
Notification RPCD.CO.RRB.No.107/03.05.33/2013-14  dated June 4, 2014
, the Reserve Bank hereby specifies that with effect from the  fortnight beginning August 9, 2014, every Regional Rural Bank shall maintain in  India assets as detailed in
notification RPCD.CO.RRB.No.35/03.05.28(B)/2009-10  dated October 29, 2009
, the value of which shall not at the close of business  of any day be less than 22.0 per cent of the total net demand and time  liabilities in India as on the last Friday of the second preceding fortnight.
