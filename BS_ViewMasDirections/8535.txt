Madam / Sir,
Term Repo under  Liquidity Adjustment Facility
As announced today  in the
Second Quarter Review of Monetary Policy 2013-14
, it has been decided to  increase the quantum of liquidity to be provided through term repos of 7-day  and 14-day tenor from the existing 0.25 per cent to 0.50 per cent of net demand  and time liabilities (NDTL) of the banking system with immediate effect.
2. All other terms  and conditions of the Term Repo under Liquidity Adjustment Facility as  stipulated in our circular
FMD.MOAG.No.89/01.01.009/2013-14 dated October 8,  2013
remain unchanged.
