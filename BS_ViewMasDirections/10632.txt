Marginal Standing Facility
As announced in the Fourth Bi-monthly Monetary Policy Statement today
, it has been decided by the Monetary Policy Committee (MPC) to reduce the Repo rate under the Liquidity Adjustment Facility (LAF) by 25 basis points from 6.50 per cent to
6.25 per cent
with immediate effect.
Consequent to the change in the Repo rate, the Marginal Standing Facility (MSF) rate will stand adjusted to
6.75 per cent
with immediate effect.
All other terms and conditions of the current MSF scheme will remain unchanged.
