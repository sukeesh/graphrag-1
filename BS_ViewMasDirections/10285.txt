Inclusion in the Second Schedule to the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 – “Vasai Vikas Sahakari Bank Ltd, Vasai Thane”
We advise that the name of “Vasai Vikas Sahakari Bank Ltd, Vasai Thane” has been included in the Second Schedule to the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 by Notification DCBR.CO.BPD.02/16.05.000/2015-16 dated December 17, 2015 published in the Gazette of India vide (Weekly No. 5 - Part III - Section 4) dated January 30, 2016.
