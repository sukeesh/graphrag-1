4. The limits for the long term investors remaining unutilized at the end of March 2017 will be released for investment under the general category in April 2017.
5. All other existing conditions, including the security-wise limits, investment of coupons being permitted outside the limits and investments being restricted to securities with a minimum residual maturity of three years, will continue to apply.
