Inclusion in the Second Schedule to the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 – ‘Rajarambapu Sahakari Bank Ltd., Peth, Sangli’
We advise that the name of ‘Rajarambapu Sahakari Bank Ltd., Peth, Sangli’ has been included in the Second Schedule to the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 by Notification DCBR.CO.BPD.05/16.05.000/2015-16 dated May 6, 2016 published in the Gazette of India (Weekly No. 24 - Part III - Section 4) dated June 11, 2016.
