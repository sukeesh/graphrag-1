Exclusion of “National Australia Bank” from the Second Schedule of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934
We advise that the “National Australia Bank” has been excluded from the Second Schedule to the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 vide Notification DBR.IBD.No.10667/23.13.085/2018-19 dated June 24, 2019 and published in the Gazette of India (
Part III - Section 4
) dated July 27, 2019 – Aug 02, 2019.
