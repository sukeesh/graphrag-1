Inclusion in the Second Schedule to the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 – Apna Sahakari Bank Ltd, Mumbai
We advise that the name of “Apna Sahakari Bank Ltd., Mumbai" has been included in the Second Schedule to the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 by Notification DCBR.CO.BPD.01/16.05.000/2015-16 dated July 28, 2015 published in the Gazette of India vide (Weekly No. 36 - Part III - Section 4) dated September 5, 2015.
