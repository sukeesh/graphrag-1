Liquidity Adjustment Facility – Repo and Reverse Repo Rates
As announced in the
Second Bi-monthly Monetary Policy Statement, 2018-19
, today, it has been decided by the Monetary Policy Committee (MPC) to increase the policy Repo rate under the Liquidity Adjustment Facility (LAF) by 25 basis points from 6.0 per cent to
6.25 per cent
with immediate effect.
2. Consequent to the change in the Repo rate, the Reverse Repo rate under the LAF stands adjusted to
6.00 per cent
with immediate effect.
3. All other terms and conditions of the extant LAF Scheme will remain unchanged.
