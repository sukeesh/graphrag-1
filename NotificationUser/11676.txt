Foreign Exchange Management (Deposit)(Amendment) Regulations, 2019
In exercise of the powers conferred by clause (f) of sub-section (3) of Section 6 and sub-section (2) of Section 47 of the Foreign Exchange Management Act, 1999 (42 of 1999) and in partial modification of its
Notification No. FEMA 5(R)/2016-RB dated April 01, 2016
, the Reserve Bank makes the following amendment in the Foreign Exchange Management (Deposit) Regulations, 2016, as amended from time to time, namely :-
2. Short title and commencement: -
(i) These Regulations may be called the Foreign Exchange Management (Deposit) (Amendment) Regulations, 2019.
(ii) They shall come into force with effect from the date of their publication in the Official Gazette.
3. Amendment of the regulations: -
Sub-regulation 3 of regulation 6 including all the words and expressions contained therein shall be deleted.
(R.K.Moolchandani)
Chief General Manager
