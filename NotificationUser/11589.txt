Inclusion in the Second Schedule to the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 – Delhi State Co-operative Bank Ltd., Delhi
We advise that the name of “Delhi State Co-operative Bank Ltd., Delhi" has been included in the Second Schedule to the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 by Notification DCBR.CO.RCBD.No.02/19.51.025/2018-19 dated April 1, 2019 published in the Gazette of India (Weekly No. 21 - Part III - Section 4) dated May 25- May 31, 2019.
