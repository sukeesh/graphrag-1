Exclusion of “Commonwealth Bank of Australia” from the Second Schedule of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934
We advise that the “
Commonwealth Bank of Australia
” has been excluded from the Second Schedule of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 vide Notification DBR.IBD.No.2223/23.13.127/ 2017-18 dated September 05, 2017, and published in the Gazette of India (
Part III - Section 4
) dated October 28- November 03, 2017.
