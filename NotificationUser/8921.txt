Special Term Repo  Auction
As announced yesterday in the
Second  Bi-Monthly Monetary Policy Statement for 2014-15
, it has been decided to introduce  special term repo auctions of 28-day tenor for a notified amount up to 0.25  per cent of net demand and time liabilities (NDTL) of the banking system. The  28-day special term repo auction will usually be conducted on non-reporting  Fridays. The first such special term repo auction will be conducted on June 6,  2014 (Friday).
2. The special term repo auctions will be conducted  as per the revised guidelines on Term Repo issued on
February 13,  2014
.
3. Please acknowledge receipt.
