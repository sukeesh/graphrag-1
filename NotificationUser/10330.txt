All Scheduled Commercial Banks (excluding RRBs), Scheduled Urban Co-operative Banks and Standalone Primary Dealers
Madam / Sir,
Liquidity Adjustment Facility – Repo and Reverse Repo Rates
As announced in the Monetary policy by the Governor today
, it has been decided to reduce the Repo rate under the Liquidity Adjustment Facility (LAF) by 25 basis points from 6.75 per cent to
6.50 per cent
.
Further, the Reverse Repo rate under the LAF has been increased by 25 basis points from 5.75 percent to
6.00 percent
.
These changes will come into force with immediate effect. All other terms and conditions of the current LAF Scheme will remain unchanged.
Yours sincerely
