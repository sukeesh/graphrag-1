Liquidity Adjustment  Facility – Repo and Reverse Repo Rates
As  announced by the Governor today
, it has been decided to reduce the Repo rate  under the Liquidity Adjustment Facility (LAF) by 25 basis points from 8.00 per  cent to
7.75 per cent
with immediate effect.
2.  Consequent to the change in the Repo rate, the Reverse Repo rate under the LAF will  stand adjusted to
6.75 per cent
with immediate effect.
3. All other terms and conditions of the current LAF Scheme  will remain unchanged.
4. Please acknowledge receipt.
Yours sincerely
