Inclusion of “National Bank of Abu Dhabi PJSC” in the Second Schedule to the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934
We advise that the
“National Bank of Abu Dhabi PJSC”
has been included in the Second Schedule to the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 vide Notification DBR.IBD. No.5196/23.03.030/2015-16 dated October 26, 2015, and published in the Gazette of India (
Part III - Section 4
) dated January 30 – February 05, 2016.
