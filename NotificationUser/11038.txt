Inclusion in the Second Schedule to the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 – Telangana State Co-operative Apex Bank Ltd., Hyderabad
We advise that the name of “Telangana State Co-operative Apex Bank Ltd., Hyderabad" has been included in the Second Schedule to the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 by Notification DCBR.CO.RCBD.No.02/19.51.025/2016-17 dated March 29, 2017 published in the Gazette of India (Weekly No. 21 - Part III - Section 4) dated May 27-June 02, 2017.
