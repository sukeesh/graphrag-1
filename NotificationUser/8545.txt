Marginal Standing Facility-Revision in timings
As announced in the
Second  Quarter Review of the Monetary Policy 2013-14
on October 29, 2013, it has  been decided to revise the timings of Marginal Standing Facility (MSF). The MSF  will now be available between 7.00 pm and 7.30 pm instead of the existing timings  of 4.45 pm to 5.15 pm. The change in timings will take effect from
November  5, 2013
.
2. All other terms and conditions of the current MSF scheme will remain  unchanged.
